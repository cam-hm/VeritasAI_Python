How RAG Works

Four-step process from document upload to intelligent answers

1

Document Upload

Upload your business documents. System extracts and processes text automatically.

2

Chunking & Embedding

Documents are split into chunks and converted to vector embeddings for semantic search.

3

Query Processing

User question is embedded and matched against document chunks using similarity search.

4

Answer Generation

AI generates contextual answer using retrieved chunks, with source citations included.
